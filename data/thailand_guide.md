# Ultimate Budget Guide to Thailand (2025 Edition)

## Currency
1 USD = ~35 Thai Baht (THB)

## Accommodation
- **Hostels**: $6-10 USD/night. Look for "Lub d" or "Slumber Party".
- **Budget Hotels**: $15-25 USD/night. Best areas: Chinatown, Ari.
- **Luxury for Less**: 5-star hotels in Bangkok often cost $80-100 USD.

## Food Costs
- **Street Food**: Pad Thai or Som Tum costs 40-60 THB ($1.50).
- **Food Courts**: Pier 21 at Terminal 21 is famous. Meals under $2.
- **Water**: 7-Eleven water costs 7-15 THB.

## Transport
- **BTS/MRT**: 16-59 THB ($0.50 - $1.70) per trip.
- **Grab vs Bolt**: Bolt is usually 30% cheaper than Grab.
- **Tuk-tuks**: Usually a tourist trap. Only use for short fun rides, negotiate hard.

## Secret Tips
- **Alcohol**: Beer is expensive in bars (100+ THB). Buy at 7-Eleven (40-50 THB).
- **Sim Cards**: Buy AIS tourist SIM at the airport, or eSIM via Airalo.
- **Temples**: Grand Palace is expensive (500 THB). Wat Arun is cheaper (100 THB) and arguably prettier at sunset.
