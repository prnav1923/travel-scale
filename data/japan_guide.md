# Budget Japan Travel Guide

## Currency
1 USD = ~150 Japanese Yen (JPY)

## Accommodation
- **Capsule Hotels**: 3000-5000 JPY ($20-35). Nine Hours is a reputable chain.
- **Business Hotels**: APA Hotels or Toyoko Inn cost 6000-9000 JPY ($40-60).
- **Manga Cafes**: Sleep for 1500-2500 JPY ($10-17) overnight.

## Food
- **Konbini**: 7-Eleven, Lawson, FamilyMart. Onigiri costs 150 JPY ($1).
- **Beef Bowl**: Yoshinoya/Matsuya bowls cost 400-600 JPY ($3-4).
- **Sushi**: Kaiten-zushi (conveyor belt) plates start at 110 JPY ($0.75).

## Transport
- **JR Pass**: Price increased in late 2023. Often cheaper to buy individual Shinkansen tickets or use regional passes.
- **Local Trains**: ~200 JPY per ride.
- **Night Bus**: Tokyo to Osaka via Willer Express costs ~4000-6000 JPY (vs 14000 JPY Shinkansen).

## Tips
- **Tax Free**: Bring your passport. Purchases over 5000 JPY (~$33) are tax-free (10% off).
- **Cash is King**: Many ramen shops and shrines only accept cash.
- **Free Views**: Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building has free observation decks.
